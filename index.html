    Find Books    

Bookr makes it easy to find books for any class you might be taking. Able to search by subject, author, or title; you'll be able to find the text that'll give you a step up in your studies.

Now that Bookr is making a statement in colleges at across the Americas, you'll be able to find relevant texts for any major or course.

    Review Books    

Once you've finished a class, it's easy to leave a review on the texts you used. Search for the book title, select how many star rating it receives, and leave a comment about your thoughts.

With each rating that a book receives, it's relevancy score rises. By rating your textbooks, you're giving the power and knowledge to others to make educated decisions on what books they'll choose.

    Save Favorites    

Hear about a good text for a class, or stumble on something interesting while searching through a catalogue? Bookr has a favorites feature that'll keep track of those for later!

Just a click of a button, and your favorites are saved to your profile ready to be viewed at any time. Search for your other student's favorite books, and see what they've saved for reference.

    Study Effectively    

Don't waste your time on poorly written, out-of-date textbooks again. This app helps you find the books that'll help you learn the material, and ace your classes.

In 2018 Bookr was able to really push students to the next level. Students reporting that they used Bookr for each class they were taking, increased 3/4 of a letter grade on their GPA.
